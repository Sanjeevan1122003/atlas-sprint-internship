REQUIREMENTS & WARNINGS

MEDICAL CLEARANCE

All participants must provide a medical certificate from a licensed physician confirming fitness for high-altitude or extreme environment activities.

TRAVEL INSURANCE

Comprehensive travel insurance including emergency evacuation coverage (minimum $100,000) is mandatory for all expeditions.

EXPERIENCE VERIFICATION

Extreme expeditions require documented prior experience. Our team will verify your climbing/trekking history during consultation.

RISK ACKNOWLEDGMENT

Extreme environments carry inherent risks including injury and death. All participants must sign comprehensive liability waivers before departure.
